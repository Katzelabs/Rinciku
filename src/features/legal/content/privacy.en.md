This Privacy Policy explains how Rinciku (the "Service"), operated by Zidan Hafiz Rapiyani ("we", "us", "our"), collects, uses, and shares your personal data. We act as the data controller for the personal data described below.

## 1. Data we collect

- **Account data** — your email address and authentication details, used to create and secure your account.
- **Financial data** — the income, expenses, categories, budgets, and currency settings you enter. This is the core data the Service exists to help you manage.
- **Preferences** — settings such as your chosen language.
- **Technical data** — basic information needed to operate the Service (for example, your authentication session).

We do not ask for, or intentionally collect, bank credentials or payment-card numbers.

## 2. How we use your data

We use your data to:

- provide, secure, and maintain the Service;
- generate AI purchase-consultation responses based on your current budget state;
- remember your preferences (such as language); and
- communicate with you about your account (for example, email confirmation and password recovery).

## 3. Legal basis

Where the laws of Indonesia (Law No. 27 of 2022 on Personal Data Protection) or other applicable data-protection laws (such as the GDPR) apply, we process your personal data on the basis of **your consent**, given when you create an account and use the Service, and as necessary to **perform our agreement** with you under the Terms of Service. You may withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account.

## 4. How your data is stored and shared

We do not sell your personal data. We share it only with the service providers ("sub-processors") needed to run Rinciku:

- **Supabase** — hosting of our database, authentication, and storage of your account and financial data.
- **OpenRouter and Google (Gemini)** — when you use the AI purchase-consultation feature, the relevant parts of your budget context (such as income, spending so far, and the purchase you are considering) are sent to these AI providers to generate a response. Do not enter information in the chat that you do not want processed by these providers.
- **Cloudflare Pages** — hosting and serving the application to your browser.

Some of these providers may process data outside your country. We rely on your consent and the providers' own safeguards for such transfers.

## 5. Cookies and local storage

Rinciku uses your browser's local storage for essential functionality only — keeping you signed in (your authentication session) and remembering your language preference. We do not use advertising or third-party tracking cookies.

## 6. Data retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, we delete or anonymize your personal data, except where we are required to retain certain information by law.
